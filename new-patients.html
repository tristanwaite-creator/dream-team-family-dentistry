New Patients

Welcome to Dream Team Family Dentistry! Here's everything you need to know for your first visit.

Your First Visit

What to Expect

We've designed your first appointment to be thorough, comfortable, and stress-free.

1

Welcome & Paperwork

Our friendly front desk team will greet you warmly. You'll complete patient forms and we'll verify your insurance coverage.

2

Comprehensive Exam

Dr. Daniel will thoroughly examine your teeth, gums, and oral tissues. We'll take X-rays and perform an oral cancer screening.

3

Professional Cleaning

Our skilled hygienist will remove plaque and tartar buildup, then provide personalized oral health tips for your home care.

4

Treatment Discussion

Dr. Daniel will discuss all findings with you, recommend any needed treatments, and provide transparent cost estimates.

Be Prepared

What to Bring

Having these items ready will help make your first visit as smooth as possible.

  • Valid photo ID
  • Insurance card (if applicable)
  • List of current medications
  • Medical history (including allergies)
  • Payment method for co-pay
  • Completed patient forms (or arrive 15 min early)
  • Previous dental X-rays (if available)
  • Referral information (if applicable)

Good to Know

Office Policies

A few things to keep in mind to help us serve you better.

Appointment Times

Please arrive 10-15 minutes early for your first visit to complete any remaining paperwork.

Cancellations

Please call at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule to ensure quality care for all patients.

Payment

Payment is due at the time of service. We accept most insurance, cash, cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
