Accessibility Statement

Dream Team Family Dentistry is committed to ensuring digital and physical accessibility for all patients.

Important: This accessibility statement is a template and should be reviewed and customized by qualified legal counsel to ensure compliance with all applicable accessibility laws and standards.

Our Commitment

Dream Team Family Dentistry is committed to providing a website and physical office that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and dental practice.

Website Accessibility

We strive to ensure our website conforms to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Our ongoing accessibility efforts include:

Visual Accessibility

Sufficient color contrast and resizable text for improved readability

Keyboard Navigation

Full keyboard accessibility for users who cannot use a mouse

Screen Reader Support

Semantic HTML and ARIA labels for assistive technologies

Responsive Design

Mobile-friendly design that works on all devices

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Skip navigation links to bypass repetitive content
  • Descriptive alt text for images
  • Clear heading structure for easy navigation
  • Visible focus indicators for keyboard users
  • Form labels and error messages that are clearly associated with inputs
  • Reduced motion option for users with vestibular disorders
  • Consistent navigation throughout the site
  • Descriptive link text that makes sense out of context

Physical Office Accessibility

Our dental office is designed to accommodate patients with various accessibility needs:

Building Access

  • Accessible parking spaces near the entrance
  • Ramp access to the building entrance
  • Wide doorways to accommodate wheelchairs and mobility devices
  • Accessible restrooms

Treatment Areas

  • Treatment rooms that can accommodate wheelchairs
  • Adjustable dental chairs for patient comfort
  • Staff trained to assist patients with mobility needs

Communication Accommodations

  • Staff who speak English, Spanish, and Arabic
  • Written materials available upon request
  • Extra time for patients who need it
  • Clear verbal communication about procedures and care

Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (such as JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation

Browser Compatibility

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest versions of the following browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Ongoing Efforts

We are committed to continuous improvement of our accessibility. Our ongoing efforts include:

  • Regular accessibility audits of our website
  • Staff training on accessibility best practices
  • Incorporating accessibility feedback from users
  • Staying current with accessibility standards and guidelines

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some content may have limitations. We are actively working to address any accessibility issues and appreciate your patience as we continue to improve.

Feedback and Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website and dental practice. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or need assistance, please contact us. We will make reasonable efforts to provide the information you need in an accessible format.

If you need any accommodations for your dental visit, please let us know when scheduling your appointment so we can best serve you.
